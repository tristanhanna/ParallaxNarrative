My BBQ Adventure

Tri-Tip

Tri-Tip is one of my favorite cuts I have cooked so far in my great BBQ experience. It has the flavor of a brisket but cooks in a quarter of the time so it is perfect for a beginner like me.

Beef Flank

Flank is a tough cut of meat but a little smoke can change that.

Sirloin Flap

Sirloin Flap is by far my favorite cut of meat I have smoked in my great adventure. It's tender and full of flavor!

Brisket

I have only made one brisket so far and it took almost 10 hours to complete. I am not sure if I want to try to cook the king of BBQ again because it was so difficult to not get it to dry out, but I was surprised with how well it turned out, and my friend got a DSLR shot of my first brisket!

Ribeye

I just figured I would share a ribeye steak I cooked recently for the end because I am out of my BBQ pictures but this one is special because the cow that made it possible came from my family's ranch. Know where your food comes from!
